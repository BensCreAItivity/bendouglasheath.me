About Me

I help organisations apply design, creativity and empathy to define what they do and the impact it has. With experience across diverse roles and industries, I bring ideas and practical thinking to help solve complex problems and make a tangible difference.

Three domains I work through:

Strategic Design: Bring people together to share ideas, unpack experiences, and find opportunities. Using design-led methods, I guide teams to create strategies and practical plans for action.

Communication: Turn complex information into clear, meaningful content and narratives that people can understand and act on. This has included design artefacts, investor and brand communications.

Emerging Technology: Explore emerging technologies and the ways they can support and enhance human thinking, creativity, and experience.

What Floats my Boat (right now)

AI (of course...): Beyond generating Ghibli inspired images of my pets, I also like to explore the role AI might play in creating better work experiences (that is here), deeper thinking and learning (this is arriving fast) and design for better (_showing promise, but it's complicated...).

Neurodiversity: I'm on a path to understand my own particular flavour of this and how those diverse neuron can better navigate a neurotypical world.

Evolving Education: I am learning and (un)learning how alternative and self-directed education might offer a better start in life path for kids.

Nature Aquariums: Who imagined a glass container filled with water and organic matter could teach you so much about life? This is a real rabbit hole.
